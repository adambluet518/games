A Tale of the Sunken Kingdom

Long ago, the Kingdom of Aeloria floated among the clouds — a civilisation of scholars, mages, and warriors who wielded the power of the Grand Aetheric Crystal, a stone of pure light that hung above their capital like a second sun.

The scholar Malachar grew obsessed with the Crystal's power. When Aeloria refused him, his obsession curdled into fury — and in one catastrophic night, he shattered it. Without the Crystal's light, Aeloria fell, sinking floor by floor into the earth, twisting and corrupting over five hundred years of darkness.

The village of Thornwall clings to the surface above the ruin, its people haunted by what lies below. They call it the Maw. Many have descended. None have returned.

You are their last hope. Descend. Reclaim the Crystal. End the darkness.
